VirtuCards

VirtuCards is a series of game applications designed to meet the needs of card game aficionado with the safety precautions of the current global pandemic in mind. My role in this project was to work on an assortment of tasks both front-end and back-end. I worked on establishing connection with the Photon servers; creating game rooms; designing UI for the cards; implementing the game logic for shuffling cards and implementing exit functionality. I worked with Unity, C#, Google Firebase and the Photon Library during this project.

History

VirtuCards was created as a part of the group's CS 307: Software Engineering I class. We were tasked with spending the entire semester creating something worthwhile and given full control of the creative process as well. The only thing we were required to do is regularly update the undergraduate teaching assistants and the professor. After getting to know one another, our group, inspired by our remote lectures, decided to create a game that allowed people to bridge the distances created by the pandemic.
